Emergency Help for Animals & Birds

Quick guidance to save lives during emergencies

Immediate Steps You Can Take

Injured Animal

Stay calm. Approach slowly and carefully. Call nearby vet or NGO immediately.

Injured Bird

Pick gently with cloth. Keep in ventilated box. Avoid giving food/water forcefully.

Call Help

Use our rescue page to alert NGOs/volunteers instantly with location & picture.

Common Emergency Scenarios

Bleeding Injury

Apply gentle pressure with clean cloth. Avoid using unclean materials.

Hit by Vehicle

Keep the animal calm and still. Cover gently and transport safely to vet.

Trapped Bird

Use a soft cloth or box. Do not try to force wings; contact volunteer/rescue teams.

Snake Bite or Dangerous Animal

Keep safe distance. Do not try to handle alone. Call expert help immediately.
