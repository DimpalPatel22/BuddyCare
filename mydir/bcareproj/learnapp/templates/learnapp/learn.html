üå¶ Seasonal Care Tips

Summer

For Birds

Keep shallow bowls of clean water on balconies, terraces, or windowsills. Avoid metal containers as they heat up. Place some grains or fruits in shaded areas.

For Animals

Provide fresh water bowls on the street or outside your home. Create shaded resting spots using cloth or cardboard. Avoid walking pets on hot concrete to prevent paw burns.

Winter

For Birds

Cover cages or resting areas with cloth at night. Place old woolens or dry leaves in corners where they perch. Ensure access to food with higher energy (like grains and nuts).

For Animals

Provide warm shelters using cardboard boxes with cloth/blankets. Street animals benefit from temporary ‚Äúcommunity shelters.‚Äù If you own a pet, avoid bathing them too often during peak cold.

Festivals

For Birds

During kite flying festivals, keep first-aid supplies ready (antiseptic, cotton). Report injured birds immediately to local NGOs. Avoid flying kites near bird nesting areas.

For Animals

During fireworks, keep pets indoors with windows closed. Play soft music to calm them. For street animals, provide temporary safe zones like basements or garages. Avoid feeding animals near crowded festival zones.
